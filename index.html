Frequently Asked Questions

How long are the books?
All of our books consist of at least seven chapters, with a minimum of four captivating images per chapter. This results in a comprehensive reading experience of approximately 35 pages, including chapter titles, for each book. With our extensive selection of titles spanning a wide range of subjects and themes, you can be sure to find the perfect book to engage your child's interest and fuel their passion for knowledge.
How many books are there?
At Brainy Bees AI, our passion for nurturing curiosity and learning knows no bounds. Our current collection encompasses 10 unique series, each featuring 12 engaging books, along with bonus books in some series. However, the world of knowledge is vast and ever-expanding, and so are our plans for the future. We are eager to continue growing our collection, and we invite you to become a part of this exciting journey. If you have ideas for additional series or books, please submit them using the form link provided below. Your creative input will contribute to the development of our library and help us inspire even more young minds to embark on their own adventures in learning.
I found a mistake in the book, can I report it?
At Brainy Bees AI, we strive for excellence and are committed to providing the best possible experience for our readers. We encourage you to report any errors or inconsistencies you may find in our books through the linked form provided. Your valuable feedback helps us improve and maintain the quality of our content. As a token of our appreciation for your contribution, we will gladly send you an updated version of the e-book, free of charge. Together, we can ensure that our books continue to inspire and educate young minds.
Can I get book updates?
At Brainy Bees AI, we are dedicated to providing an ever-evolving learning experience for our readers. We understand that knowledge and technology advance rapidly, and we want to ensure that our content remains up-to-date and relevant. That's why when you purchase any of our books, you'll automatically receive all future updates in the form of an e-book at no additional cost. This way, you can be confident that your child's learning journey with Brainy Bees AI will always be enriched with the latest information and discoveries, helping them stay ahead and nurture their curiosity.

At Brainy Bees AI, we understand the importance of providing accessible educational resources to schools and other learning institutions. To support educators in their mission to inspire young minds, we are pleased to offer exclusive discounts on subscriptions to complete series for classrooms and educational programs. Reach out to us to learn more about our special offers and discover how Brainy Bees AI can help enhance your educational environment.
