What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are stored on the devices by the means of which the visitors access the internet pages. The internet pages can store (for a limited time) various user preferences and actions (e.g. site language, font size, login credentials etc.) in these files so that the user is not required to enter them again on the next visit.

Cookie Types

Cookies can be divided into two basic types according to their lifespan.

  • Short-term (session cookie) — they remain in your browser until you close your browser, then they are deleted.
  • Long-term (persistent cookies) — they remain stored in the browser for a long time (depending on your browser settings and cookie settings) or until you delete them manually.
